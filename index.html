About

I am a strongly driven and inherently ambitious person with an aptitude for photography and computing. From a young age, I was fascinated with how things worked and would constantly question objects in my environment which sparked a pursuit of technical knowledge. This pursuit continued and matured at school into a desire for success as I began to delve into business, economics and politics to craft a good understanding of the world and how things operate in it. During this time at college, I decided to major in computing as it better supported my hobbies at the time which included computer hardware, programming and media content creation.

After turning 18 and getting into the University of Lincoln my ambition only grew with independence as I started travelling up and down the country, which is where my infatuation with photography came from as I was finding phone cameras were just unable to capture what I was seeing. From this time travelling, I have managed to complete the national three peaks challenge as well as the famous north coast 500 road trip.

My time at university has been compelling as I have picked up many new skills in the branches of computer science which have included developing games & programs, creating an artificial intelligence system, various group work tasks and machine learning algorithms which pushed my ability and grew my confidence in picking up new skills. I would often be given a task in various modules, that would run simultaneously, which required me to learn a different programming language or software and apply it to solve problems commonly found in the industry.

During the Covid-19 pandemic, it has been very hard to expand my portfolio and show my true potential. I had planned to print business cards and dive into car photography which sadly was not possible. However, I would constantly be out when I could albeit on walks for exercise or taking pictures of my friends cars whenever the chance came up. I have done a couple of commissioned shoots for clients who have been extremely satisfied with what I had produced. I am currently studying a masters degree in computer science while keeping up with photography and videogrophy as i can while the world and job climate recovers from the pandemic. I have recently purchased my first car and motorbike which has now got me keen on mechanics, of which i already have a somewhat good starting knowladge considering my dad is a self taught mechanic and classic car enthusiast.
